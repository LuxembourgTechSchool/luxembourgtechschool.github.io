In ESERO Luxembourg's 2023 National CanSat Competition, LTS students participated across two teams: LTS SpaceCan and LTS-GalacticMappers.

![LTS Students with two prizes at the Science Center](/images/news/cansat-2023.jpeg)

{:.image-caption} 
*LTS Students with two prices at the Science Center*

Both teams were awarded prizes for their performance at the competition LTS-GalacticMappers won the **"Best Outreach"** award, highlighting the team's exceptional work in engaging with the community and effectively communicating their project. LTS SpaceCan was acknowledged with the **"Outstanding Science Mission"** award for their work.
