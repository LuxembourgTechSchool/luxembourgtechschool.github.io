More than 150 students from 5e, 6e and 7e came during the week and tried to solve the smart house challenge.

![High school students working on fixing the smart house.](/images/news/sciencefestival-2023.JPG)

{:.image-caption} 
*High school students working on fixing the smart house.*

What is it? This small IOT device simulates a smart house and has sensors, doors, windows and other electronics.
But, it has been hacked, the goal is to regain control of the smart house by solving programming challenges.
