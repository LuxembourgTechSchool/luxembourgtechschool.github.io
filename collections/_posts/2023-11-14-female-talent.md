*An amazing group of talented people, ready to tackle the world of FinTech*

Today, in collaboration with the FNR and the LHoFT, the Luxembourg Tech School is happy to announce a new program seeking to develop and strengthen female talent in the fields of finance and technology. Over the next few months, over 20 women will participate in a program that puts them in contact with some of the biggest names in Luxembourg's financial and technology industries.

Through handson sessions with these mentors, mentees will develop a variety of skills necessary to succeed in these multifaceted fields, and will work together to create a viable business proposition, tailored to Luxembourg's unique FinTech ecosystem.
