This new after-school program offers a fun and gamified learning experience for high-school students in Luxembourg aged 14 to 18.
During 4 months, from 3rd of March to 30th of June, 2022, you will learn to create 3D artworks.
From modelling and texturing to lighting and rendering, you’ll gain new creative skills and learn about the latest technologies related to digital arts, such as non-fungible tokens (NFTs).

Just like the regular LTS curriculum, this program uses a project-based approach. You work on your own ideas, projects and in groups.

Classes are online, 100% free and take place every Thursday at 17h00.

Additionally, there will be several in-person events, called “Creator’s Marathon”, where we all meet and create together. The program closes with an epic 2-day Hackathon.
