LTS Online 2022

LTS Online has launched!
Learn to create 3D art!

Register

This new after-school program offers a fun and gamified learning experience for high-school students aged 14 to 18.
During 4 months, March to June, you will learn to create 3D artworks.
From modelling and texturing to lighting and rendering, you’ll gain new creative skills and learn about the latest technologies related to digital arts, such as non-fungible token (NFTs).

Just like the regular LTS curriculum, this program uses a project-based approach. You work on your own ideas, projects and in groups.

Classes are online, 100% free and take place every Wednesday at 17h00.

Additionally, there will be several in-person events, called “Creator’s Marathon”, where we all meet and create together. The program closes with an epic 2-day Hackathon.

Details

  • Age requirements: 14 to 18.
  • Period: March to June
  • Language: English
  • You need a computer / laptop and a working Internet connection.
  • Deadline to register: 20th February
  • Places are limited to 80 students
