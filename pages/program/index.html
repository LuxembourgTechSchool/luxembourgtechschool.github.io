Discover The Program

This epic program is for all 12-19 years old students who are passionate about the digital world and eager to learn and apply technology in a real business and creative context.

We offer a 3 year program. You can start with Level G0, Level One or Level Up, depending on your age. Each level is divided into up to 3 modules, each centered on one specific domain.

At the end of each course, you will have the opportunity to present your project to the real world!

The program below is a preview of the cool topics that are covered. There won't be much theory (a little is always useful). It is filled with practical examples, exercices and you work on your own project!

During Level G0 you will discover the creative side of coding and how to talk to machines. During Level One and Level Up, you will learn a lot about teamwork, software development, entrepreneurship and even upgrade your presentation skills by multiple factors.

It won't be easy, but at the end you will have created many awesome projects, will have learned a ton of new things and, most importantly, while having a lot of fun.
